Our Mission

We connect health-conscious consumers directly with local farmers growing food using traditional methods, eliminating middlemen and providing access to products that are 100% healthier than commercial alternatives.

The Founder's Story

2011

FarmFreshApp was born from a personal awakening. Living in Florida, the Sunshine State, our founder was certain that buying food labeled organic and non-GMO would ensure their family was eating natural foods.

2021

The pivotal moment came when they discovered that "organic" milk left in a hot car trunk for several days remained "fresh" – revealing the disconnect between consumer expectations and commercial reality. As they put it: "I was scammed. That was enough. I could no longer lie to myself and feed my family this junk food anymore.

2022

This experience catalyzed research into truly natural food sources, leading to connections with local farmers in Marion County, Florida. Through conversations with these farmers, our founder identified significant challenges they faced in reaching consumers directly and receiving fair compensation for their products.

2024

The solution was clear: create a marketplace that connects local farmers directly with customers, providing access to truly natural food while supporting the farmers who grow it.

Our Vision

We envision a transformed food system where direct connections between farmers and consumers are the norm, not the exception. A system where:

  • Farmers are recognized and compensated fairly for growing truly natural food
  • Consumers have transparent access to information about how their food is grown
  • Communities are strengthened through local food economies
  • Traditional growing methods are preserved and celebrated
  • Health is improved through access to genuinely nutritious food

Make America Healthy Again

Real Food

We believe in food grown the old way – the time-tested way – like 50+ years ago, without chemicals, poison, questionable "organic" labels, or GMOs that we have become accustomed to.

American Heroes

Our farmers are not just our partners – they are our American heroes! They are growing real natural food for the people, their families, and their neighbors.

Community Building

We're building communities internally, along with the economy for the city, state, North America, and eventually the whole world.
