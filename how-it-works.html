How It Works

Our three-sided marketplace connects customers, farmers, and delivery drivers to create a seamless farm-to-table experience.

Fresh food at your fingertips

Download the App

Available for both iOS and Android devices, our user-friendly app puts local farm-fresh food at your fingertips.

Location

Let the app detect your location or enter the address manually. You can create up to 5 delivery addresses.

Browse local farm products

Explore profiles of local farmers, learn about their growing practices, and discover their available products.

Select Your Products

Choose from a variety of truly natural products, from fresh produce to eggs, meat, dairy, and more.

Place an order

Select your preferred delivery time or pickup option that fits your schedule.

Receive Fresh Products

Your order will be delivered straight from the farm to your door, keeping your produce fresh.
